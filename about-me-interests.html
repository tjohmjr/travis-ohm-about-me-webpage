My Interests

Throughout all the years I played I only ever was a first baseman and catcher. I played high school JV and Varsity for two years each, and I also played on a competitive prospects team. I met my best friends even to this day through baseball.

The gym has become a very therapeutic thing in my life. I originally started lifting weights to get stronger for baseball, but then over time I started lifting to get stronger in general and for stress relief. Now, I look forward to going to the gym every day.

We both really enjoy listening and playing various types of songs. We also enjoyed the idea of entertaining others. We still continue to DJ to this day.

During my senior year of high school I took a coding class. It primarily taught the basics of computers and the language HTML. The teacher who taught the class was the main reason I enjoy coding and decided to take computer science in college. He was always so helpful and encouraging. I excelled in that class and since them I have been doing personal projects on my own and trying to learn everything I can.
